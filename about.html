🎓 My Journey

Hello! I'm Sonu Kumar, a committed student of Data Science who is passionate about using data to solve real-world problems and extract meaningful insights. I'm currently pursuing dual Bachelor's degrees - Civil Engineering from Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology and Data Science from the prestigious IIT Madras.

My journey into technology started with curiosity about how data can transform decision-making. This led me to explore the fascinating intersection of data science and web development, where I create powerful and user-friendly applications that make complex data accessible to everyone.

As a Top 15 Finalist in the Google Cloud Agentic Hackathon, I've proven my ability to develop innovative solutions using cutting-edge AI and cloud technologies. This achievement reflects my commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I'm deeply passionate about education and mentoring. As a JEE tutor on Superprof, I help aspiring engineers navigate the challenging landscape of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, providing personalized strategies that have helped over 100 students achieve their dreams.

I believe in the power of technology to create positive change. Whether it's developing AI-powered healthcare solutions like Jeevant.ai for MIT, creating face recognition attendance systems, or building intuitive web applications, each project is an opportunity to make a meaningful impact.

My diverse experience spans from being a Campus Ambassador at E-Cell IIT Bombay to working as an Investor Relations Assistant at JITSIE IIT Madras, giving me a unique perspective on both technical innovation and business strategy.
